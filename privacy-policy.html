MoodSnap

Privacy Policy — Last updated: April 2, 2026

MoodSnap ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal mood tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data We Collect

MoodSnap stores all personal data locally on your device only. We do not operate servers or databases that store your personal information.

  • Your name — entered voluntarily in Settings, stored only on your device.
  • Mood entries — mood, optional note, date/time, and wellbeing scores you log. Stored locally.
  • Health data — if you connect Health Connect (Android), the app reads step count, sleep duration, and heart rate. This data is read at check-in time and stored locally. It is never transmitted.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, mood entries, or journal notes on any server.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs that track your behavior.

3. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party SDKs that may collect limited device data independently:

Google AdMob
MoodSnap displays banner and interstitial advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with Google's privacy policy.
Google Privacy Policy

RevenueCat
The app includes the RevenueCat SDK for managing in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect anonymized purchase and device data.
RevenueCat Privacy Policy

4. Health & Fitness Data

MoodSnap may request access to Android Health Connect to read step count, sleep sessions, and heart rate data. This data is used solely to pre-fill your daily wellbeing tracker and is never uploaded, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

You can revoke Health Connect permissions at any time in your device's Health Connect settings.

5. Children's Privacy

MoodSnap is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling MoodSnap or clearing the app's storage in your device settings. We have no ability to access or recover this data once deleted.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
